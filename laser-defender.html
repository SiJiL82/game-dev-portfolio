Laser Defender

19 August 2020

A top down space shooter game I made as part of a C# and Unity fundamentals course.

This game was a tutorial in the GameDev.tv "Complete C# Unity Developer 2D" course.

Additional features I added include positional audio, and health rewards for completing enemy waves.

Controls:

W, A, S, D keys to move around.
Space to fire.
